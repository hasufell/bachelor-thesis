Sendmail, Inc. Commitment

Sendmail, Inc. offers a commercial-grade platform that is vendor-agnostic and allows diverse, best-of-breed applications to be managed in a unified framework, the Sentrion Email Integration Platform. Sentrion helps reduce the time spent managing and integrating point solutions and vastly enhances functionality and performance.

While the Sentrion platform is specifically designed with large, complex environments in mind, we make a subset of that solution available as an open source offering. For over 25 years we have championed the belief that everyone should have access to the sophisticated tools and technologies required to run a more secure and efficient email environment. This includes companies that are too small to afford, the scholars who need a platform to experiment on, and the students and heroes who have not yet convinced their companies to modernize their infrastructure with an industry recognized platform like Sentrion.

Sentrion is not for everyone, but if you are using open source for a large complex environment and need an enterprise platform that will enable your messaging roadmap for years to come (virtualization, consolidation, cloud migration, etc.), download the product specs or request a demo to see if Sentrion is right for you.
